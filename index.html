NIKE

After extensive research work and a detailed study of Nike's brand manuals, I designed and programmed an email marketing campaign using HTML Tables. The goal was to facilitate the company's communication with its customers.

For this project it was important to capture the brand's personality, so it was a priority to stay true to its famous slogan "Just do it". That is the premise that runs through all the pieces and encourages customers to become part of the community and start enjoying its benefits from the beginning.
Date: August 2022
Client: Nike
Role: UX/UI Research & Development.
